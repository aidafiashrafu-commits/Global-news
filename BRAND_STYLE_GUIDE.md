## Brand Overview

Global News delivers trusted, original news from around the world with a focus on African stories, business insights, technology trends, sports coverage, health information, and entertainment news.

### **Vision**
To become the most trusted news source for African and global audiences seeking quality journalism and diverse perspectives.

### **Brand Values**
- **Trust**: Honest, accurate, fact-based reporting
- **Excellence**: High-quality writing and design
- **Inclusivity**: Stories from diverse perspectives
- **Innovation**: Modern presentation and technology
- **Accessibility**: Easy to read and navigate

### **Brand Personality**
- Professional yet approachable
- Modern and forward-thinking
- Bold and confident
- Trustworthy and credible
- Reader-focused

## Typography

### **Font Family**

#### **Headlines & Titles**
**Georgia** (serif)
- Weight: Bold (700)
- Size: 24-48px depending on context
- Line height: 1.3
- Letter spacing: -0.5px

**Fallback**: Georgia, serif

#### **Body Text**
**System Font Stack** (sans-serif)
```css
font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 
  "Segoe UI", Roboto, "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif;
```
- Weight: Regular (400)
- Size: 14-16px
- Line height: 1.6
- Letter spacing: 0px

#### **Monospace (Code)**
```css
font-family: "Courier New", Courier, monospace;
```

### **Type Scale**

| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Line Height |
|---------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| H1 (Hero) | Georgia | 48px | Bold | 1.3 |
| H2 (Section) | Georgia | 36px | Bold | 1.3 |
| H3 (Subsection) | Georgia | 28px | Bold | 1.4 |
| H4 (Minor) | Georgia | 24px | Bold | 1.4 |
| H5 | Georgia | 20px | Bold | 1.5 |
| H6 | Georgia | 16px | Bold | 1.5 |
| Body Text | System | 16px | Regular | 1.6 |
| Small Text | System | 14px | Regular | 1.6 |
| Caption | System | 12px | Regular | 1.5 |

### **Typography Guidelines**

**Headlines**
- Use Georgia serif font
- Bold weight (700)
- All caps for section headers
- Proper capitalization for article titles
- Maximum line length: 60 characters

**Body Text**
- Use system font for readability
- Regular weight (400)
- Line height minimum 1.6
- Line length: 45-75 characters
- Paragraph spacing: 1.5em

**Emphasis**
- **Bold**: For important words only
- *Italic*: For quotes, emphasis, foreign words
- CAPS: For highlights and labels
- Avoid underline (use color instead)

**Links**
- Color: #C40000 (News Red)
- Underline: On hover only
- Font weight: Regular
- Text decoration: None (default)

## Imagery

### **Image Types & Usage**

#### **Featured Images (Hero)**
- **Size**: 1200√ó600px (2:1 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPEG (high quality, <200KB)
- **Quality**: High contrast, professional photography
- **Content**: News-relevant, impactful
- **Orientation**: Landscape preferred

#### **Article Thumbnails**
- **Size**: 400√ó300px (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPEG (optimized)
- **Quality**: Clear and recognizable
- **Content**: Crop most important part
- **File size**: <50KB

#### **Social Media**
- **Size**: 1200√ó630px (1.9:1 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG
- **Content**: Include headline text
- **File size**: <200KB
- **Safe zone**: Inner 1050√ó560px

#### **Category Images**
- **Size**: 300√ó300px (square)
- **Format**: JPEG or PNG
- **Quality**: Distinctive and relevant
- **File size**: <30KB

### **Image Quality Standards**

‚úì **DO:**
- Use high-quality original photos
- Ensure proper color reproduction
- Include alt text for accessibility
- Optimize for web (compress)
- Use appropriate aspect ratios
- Provide captions for important images

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Use watermarked or low-quality images
- Distort or stretch images
- Use overly filtered/edited photos
- Forget alt text
- Use images without rights
- Upload uncompressed files

### **Image Editing Guidelines**

**Color Correction**
- Maintain natural colors
- Adjust contrast for clarity
- Ensure consistent white balance
- Avoid oversaturation

**Cropping**
- Follow rule of thirds
- Keep subject in focus
- Leave breathing room
- Maintain aspect ratios

**Filters**
- Minimal use (subtle only)
- Must maintain professionalism
- No cartoon or watercolor effects
- Black & white acceptable for archives

### **Attribution & Rights**

- Always use images with appropriate rights
- Credit photographers in captions when required
- Use royalty-free stock for supplementary images
- Avoid copyright infringement
- Document source for compliance

## Voice & Tone

### **Brand Voice Characteristics**

**Professional**: Credible, authoritative, knowledgeable
**Approachable**: Friendly, conversational, relatable
**Direct**: Clear, concise, no jargon
**Engaging**: Interesting, compelling, relevant
**Accurate**: Fact-based, unbiased, verified

### **Tone Guidelines by Context**

#### **Headlines**
- **Compelling**: Create interest without clickbait
- **Accurate**: Never mislead readers
- **Direct**: Lead with the main point
- **Active voice**: Use action verbs
- **Specific**: Avoid vague language

**Examples:**
‚úì "How African Tech Startups Are Disrupting Silicon Valley"
‚úì "Breaking: New Cancer Treatment Shows 95% Success Rate"
‚úì "Why Global Markets Are Watching This Election Closely"

‚ùå "You Won't Believe What Happened Next"
‚ùå "This One Weird Trick..."
‚ùå "Doctors Hate This..."

#### **Articles**
- **Informative**: Provide context and details
- **Balanced**: Present multiple perspectives
- **Engaging**: Tell the story effectively
- **Professional**: Maintain credibility
- **Clear**: Use simple language

#### **Captions**
- **Descriptive**: Explain what's in the image
- **Context**: Provide relevant background
- **Source**: Credit photographer/source
- **Concise**: Keep to 1-2 sentences

#### **Social Media**
- **Conversational**: Friendly, engaging tone
- **Short**: Respect platform character limits
- **Shareable**: Encourage discussion
- **Visual**: Use emojis sparingly
- **Timely**: Reference current events

### **Grammar & Style Standards**

**Capitalization**
- Headlines: Title Case (capitalize main words)
- Categories: Title Case
- Menu items: Title Case

**Numbers**
- One to nine: Spell out
- 10+: Use numerals
- Percentages: Use % symbol (no space)
- Dates: January 15, 2026 format

**Abbreviations**
- First mention: Spell out fully with abbreviation in parentheses
- Subsequent: Use abbreviation only
- Common: US, UK, AI (no periods)

**Punctuation**
- Serial comma: Use (Oxford comma)
- Em dash: ‚Äî (with spaces)
- Quotes: Use "curly quotes" (not straight)
- Ellipsis: ... (three dots, no spaces)
